Frequent questions

Lasso is a complete music downloader that use JSON-based packages to download any song you want through our desktop app. As a user, i add to the current package the song that i want to download, then i go to the package section and download it. I open the desktop app to open the downloaded JSON and click on download button. It's all, your songs will start to be downloaded. Yes, the fetched data for Lasso is brought from the most used music stream platforms (Spotify and Deezer) and the audio from Youtube.
